Our Story

A Legacy Born in Kashmir, Rooted in the UAE

Reshie Handicrafts began its journey in 1982 with a simple dream — to bring authentic craftsmanship and heartfelt tradition to people far from home. Founded by Shahzad Ahamad Reshie, who journeyed from Kashmir to the UAE, the vision was built on hard work, honesty, and passion.

For over 44 years in this country, he has dedicated his life to growing Reshie Handicrafts from a humble beginning into a family of shops known for unique gifts, cultural treasures, and handmade beauty. Today, we proudly welcome you to visit any of our branches and be part of our story.
